# 🧑🍳 How to Contribute

## Become a Tester

### Leave Feedback

Join the [Discord](https://discord.gg/NM5HakA9nC) and ask for the Tester Role. Test the app for bugs, features and provide feedback on the beta-testnet channel. Provide screenshots, video record, transaction hashes when possible.

### Have an idea?

We are especially open to new suggestions or better ways of doing things. If you have an idea feel free to use the appropriate channel to discuss your idea. Get the community engage with your idea and get feedback on it.

## Build &#x20;

### Developers

If you're a developer we need your help! Before you begin: Familiarize yourself with Candide Wallet by becoming a tester. Familiarize yourself with its infrastructure by going through our github account (we need technical documentation to link it here!)

You can see our progress our Github issues and you can immediately begin contributing where you see fit. We recommend checking out issues tagged as `good first issue` and recommend sticking to the respective repository's coding guidelines (linting, formatting, PRs, etc). It's that simple 🙂. You can also join us on the #dev channel in [Discord](https://discord.gg/NM5HakA9nC).

If you are looking to build on top of Candide contracts or use its infrastructure, please react out and we will be happy to get you setup

### Documentation Writers

Candide needs your help for documentation. You can see our progress on the [Github Issues](https://github.com/candidelabs/candide-docs) where you can look through whatever that interests you. Jump in our discord and talk to us first before starting to work to make sure no else is also working on the same piece of writing.  Before contributing: please en that your PRs adhere to our [Writing Style Guide](https://hackmd.io/dXQecpkJQX6XRy4y7k7j3g).
